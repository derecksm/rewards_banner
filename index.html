Dear Rewards Member
Thank you for being part of the iHerb Rewards program. You currently have $100 or more in your Rewards balance and are eligible to cash out. However, cashouts for your country are not currently supported through our new cashout process with Hyperwallet and we will need to send your funds via wire transfer. Each transfer incurs a $10 processing fee and will automatically be deducted from your balance. In the event the wire transfer is returned from the bank for any reason, an additional $25 return fee will be deducted from your balance.

In order to cash out, please email the following to rewards@iherb.com
  • Payment Address
  • Bank information (routing and account number)
  • Tax form (W-8 BEN for individuals, W-8BEN-E for businesses)
  • Rewards code
Once we’ve received your information, you’ll be cashed out automatically at the end of each month if your Rewards balance is $100 or more. Should any of your banking information change, let us know at least 2 weeks prior to the end of the month to refrain from incurring additional fees.

If you have any further questions, please contact us at rewards@iherb.com (or visit our FAQs page)
